# Sample Q&A — Groww MF Assistant

## About
These are sample queries and responses from the Groww MF Assistant chatbot.
All answers are facts-only, sourced from official HDFC Mutual Fund documents
and Groww scheme pages. No investment advice is provided.

Chatbot URL: https://groww-mf-chatbot.vercel.app

---

## Factual Queries

**Q1: What is the expense ratio of HDFC Mid Cap Fund?**

Response:
The expense ratio of HDFC Mid Cap Fund is 1.53%. This is based on the data
provided in the Scheme Information Document (SID) of HDFC Mid Cap Fund.
The expense ratio may be subject to change as per the fund's performance
and regulatory requirements.

Source: https://www.hdfcfund.com/mutual-funds/fund-documents/kim
Last updated from sources: April 2025

---

**Q2: What is the exit load for HDFC ELSS Tax Saver?**

Response:
The exit load details for HDFC ELSS Tax Saver are available in the KIM
document. For complete details, please refer to para 'Exit Load' on
page 107 of the KIM document.

Source: https://www.hdfcfund.com/mutual-funds/fund-documents/kim
Last updated from sources: April 2025

---

**Q3: What is the minimum SIP for HDFC Large Cap Fund?**

Response:
The minimum SIP amount for HDFC Large Cap Fund is available in the KIM
document. For complete details, please refer to Minimum Application Amount,
Plans and Options on pages 104 to 106 of the KIM document.

Source: https://www.hdfcfund.com/mutual-funds/fund-documents/kim
Last updated from sources: April 2025

---

**Q4: What is the lock-in period for HDFC ELSS Tax Saver?**

Response:
The lock-in period for HDFC ELSS Tax Saver is 3 years. This is a statutory
lock-in as per the scheme details. The lock-in period starts from the date
of issuance of units and is subject to meeting regulatory requirements.

Source: https://www.hdfcfund.com/mutual-funds/fund-documents/kim
Last updated from sources: April 2025

---

**Q5: What is the riskometer of HDFC Focused Fund?**

Response:
The HDFC Focused Fund invests in large-cap, mid-cap, and small-cap stocks
with a maximum of 30 stocks. The benchmark index for HDFC Focused Fund is
NIFTY 500 Total Returns Index (TRI) as per AMFI Tier I Benchmark.

Source: https://www.hdfcfund.com/mutual-funds/fund-documents/kim
Last updated from sources: April 2025

## Edge Case Handling

**Q8: Out of scope AMC query**
Query: What is the expense ratio of SBI Small Cap Fund?

Response:
I can only answer factual questions about HDFC Mutual Fund schemes listed
on Groww. Please ask about expense ratios, exit loads, SIP amounts,
lock-in periods, or fund managers.

Known limitation: The assistant currently handles out-of-scope AMC queries
by redirecting to HDFC context rather than cleanly refusing. A future
improvement would be to add explicit AMC detection in the safety filter.
